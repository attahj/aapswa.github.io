Who We Are

Afghan Association
of Puget Sound

The Afghan Association of Puget Sound represents thousands of Afghan families who have made the greater Seattle area their home. From longtime residents to recently resettled families, our community is vibrant, resilient, and deeply committed to one another.

For years, we have gathered in homes, parks, and borrowed halls. Now, we are working toward something permanent — a dedicated space where every Afghan family in our region can find community, support, and belonging.

Our Vision

The Afghan Community Center

We are planning and fundraising for a permanent community center (10,000–15,000 sq ft) in the North Seattle / South Snohomish County corridor to serve Afghan families across the region. This will be a place of gathering, learning, healing, and joy.

Education & Tutoring

After-school programs, language classes in Dari and Pashto, and adult literacy support.

Community Dining Hall

A gathering space for Eid celebrations, community gatherings, and weekly activities.

Cultural Arts Center

Space for traditional Afghan heritage, storytelling, and Islamic history.

Career & Business Services

Job placement assistance, small business support, and professional development workshops.

Youth & Family Programs

Childcare, youth sports, mentorship programs, and family counseling services.

Resettlement Support

Assistance navigating housing, healthcare, legal resources, and government services.
